Slurry Pumps

Request a QuoteBack to Products

Overview

Durable slurry transfer for demanding plant duties.

Slurry pumps are specified for moving abrasive process streams between milling, classification, leaching and tailings areas. SA Autec Industries can help match pump type, materials and capacity to the actual site duty.

The focus is on practical reliability, maintainability and integration with the surrounding plant layout.

Typical Applications

  • Mill discharge and process transfer
  • Leach and tailings circuits
  • Plant expansions and pump replacements
