Pin Mill

Compact milling equipment for size reduction, sample preparation and process feed conditioning.

Request a QuoteBack to Products

Overview

Controlled size reduction for plant and workshop use.

The pin mill supports applications where consistent particle size reduction is required before further processing, testing or material handling. It is suited to compact plant environments and can be discussed as part of a larger equipment package.

SA Autec Industries can assist with sizing, feed requirements and integration with supporting hoppers, guards and discharge arrangements.

Typical Applications

  • Sample and feed preparation
  • Fine size reduction duties
  • Small processing plant equipment packages
