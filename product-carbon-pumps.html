Carbon Pumps

Request a QuoteBack to Products

Overview

Reliable carbon movement for gold recovery circuits.

Carbon pumps support the movement of loaded, barren or regenerated carbon between tanks and process stages. SA Autec Industries can assist with pump selection and integration into new or existing gold recovery plants.

Units can be specified around duty requirements, piping layout, wear considerations and the practical needs of site operation and maintenance.

Typical Applications

  • CIL and CIP plant carbon transfer
  • Gold recovery plant upgrades
  • Modular plant pump packages
