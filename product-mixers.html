Overview

Engineered for maximum mixing efficiency and long service life.

The Autec Heavy-Duty Agitator is purpose-designed and manufactured to deliver reliable, efficient slurry mixing in gold leaching, adsorption, and conditioning applications. Built specifically for the demanding conditions encountered in CIL, CIP, and other mineral processing plants, our agitators are engineered to maximise mixing efficiency while ensuring long service life and minimal maintenance.

Available in a range of sizes with 2.2 kW to 15 kW drive motors, each agitator is individually designed to suit the specific tank diameter, slurry characteristics, and process requirements of the application. Every unit features a rugged structural frame, precision-machined components, and a heavy-duty drive system to ensure dependable operation under continuous duty.

Autec's rubber-lined impeller blades are designed to provide optimum slurry suspension, oxygen transfer, and reagent mixing while reducing wear and extending component life. Whether installed in a new plant or as a replacement for existing equipment, our agitators deliver proven performance and excellent reliability.
