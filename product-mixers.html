Overview

Mixing equipment for stable plant performance.

Mixers are used across mineral processing plants where reagents, slurry or process material must be kept in motion and properly conditioned. SA Autec Industries can support mixer selection for tank geometry, material characteristics and process objectives.

Mixers can be supplied for standalone duties or included in broader modular plant and fabrication packages.

Typical Applications

  • Leach and conditioning tanks
  • Reagent make-up systems
  • Process plant fabrication packages
