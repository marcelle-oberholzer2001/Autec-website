Overview

Heavy-duty heating engineered for gold recovery.

The Autec Stainless Steel Elution Boiler is a heavy-duty, high-performance heating solution specifically designed and manufactured for gold elution and regeneration circuits. Engineered for reliability, efficiency, and ease of operation, the boiler provides a dependable source of hot water for elution processes in both permanent and remote mining operations.

Available in electric, diesel-fired, or dual-operation configurations, the boiler offers the flexibility to suit sites with reliable grid power as well as off-grid locations where diesel is the primary energy source. Built entirely from high-grade stainless steel, it provides excellent corrosion resistance, long service life, and low maintenance requirements in demanding mining environments.

Designed with operator safety and process efficiency in mind, the Autec Elution Boiler incorporates quality components, robust insulation, and precision temperature control to ensure consistent heating performance and optimum gold recovery.
