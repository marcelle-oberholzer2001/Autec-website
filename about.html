Our Story

Engineering solutions built for African conditions.

SA Autec Industries was founded with a clear purpose: to deliver mining and mineral processing equipment that actually works in the African environment. Too often, off-the-shelf international solutions arrive on African mine sites ill-suited to local conditions, logistical realities, and operational budgets.

We design and manufacture our equipment locally, drawing on hands-on experience in South African and broader African gold processing operations. Every agitator, boiler, pump, and plant we build is engineered for the specific duty it will face, from remote alluvial operations to large-scale CIL processing facilities.

Our team combines deep metallurgical process knowledge with practical fabrication and commissioning experience, ensuring every solution we deliver is fit for purpose and backed by real support.
