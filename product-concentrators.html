Overview

Improve recovery through efficient mineral upgrading.

SA Autec Industries supplies concentrator solutions for processing circuits where valuable minerals need to be separated and upgraded from feed material. Concentrators can be matched to project throughput, ore characteristics and downstream recovery requirements.

These systems are suitable for modular plants, pilot operations and production environments that need a practical recovery-focused concentration step.

Typical Applications

  • Gold and heavy mineral recovery circuits
  • Pre-concentration before leaching
  • Modular processing plant upgrades
