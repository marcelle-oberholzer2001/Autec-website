Overview

Containerized gold recovery plant for staged production.

The 4500 tpm CIL containerized plant is intended for compact gold recovery operations where transportability, commissioning speed and operating discipline are important. The modular format supports practical installation on remote sites while keeping major process areas organized and accessible.

The plant can be discussed as a full project package including layout, equipment selection, fabrication, installation support and commissioning planning.

Typical Applications

  • Small to medium gold recovery operations
  • Remote or staged mine developments
  • Fast-track modular plant deployments
